MOU Compliance Rules:

1. Purpose and Scope:
   - The MOU must clearly define the purpose of the collaboration.
   - The scope of activities should be well documented.

2. Parties and Responsibilities:
   - Each party must have clearly assigned responsibilities.
   - Joint responsibilities must be explicitly mentioned.

3. Confidentiality:
   - Confidential information must not be shared with third parties.
   - A clear process for handling confidential info must exist.

4. Term and Termination:
   - The term of the MOU must be specified.
   - Termination clauses must include notice period and conditions.

5. Dispute Resolution:
   - There should be a method for resolving disputes, such as arbitration.
   - Jurisdiction must be defined.

6. Governing Law:
   - The governing law for the MOU must be specified.
